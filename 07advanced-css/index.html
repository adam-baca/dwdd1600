About Atlanta

Atlanta is an AKC registered, 20 month old, part english cream golden retriever. She has a light coat with a golden shade on top. We got her as a puppy and she has been the best companion dog ever since.
She is however, nothing more as far as dog roles go. She is a little too crazy to be a service or therapy dog, and too big and goofy to be a show dog. But as a companion, she is perfect and her one job is "ball". She is very good at her job.

The Essentials

Schedule

Atlanta Eats 2 times a day, once in the morning, and once at night. She needs to go out at least 3 times a day, she'll let you know if she needs more.
Her Schedule is as follows:

  1. Breakfast after waking up
  2. Take her out to the bathroom
  3. Another bathroom break around noon
  4. Depending on the season, a trip to the dog park (later in the day during summer)
  5. Dinner around 9 pm
  6. One last bathroom break before bed

Activities

Atlanta is a very lazy pup, but loves to get out and explore whenever you want to. She loves walking, hiking and most importantly FETCH.

Where do I take her on walks?
In the neighborhood or to a park, she must be leashed.
Where can I take her for a treat?
Anywhere that serves pup cups. If you go somewhere for morning coffee, get a treat for yourself too
Where can I take her to play fetch?
You can take her to any park off leash and she won't wander as long as you are playing fetch with her. She will be laser focused on the ball.

Her favorite place for fetch is the dog park:

Art Dye Dog Park 1000 N 550 E St,
American Fork, UT 84003
