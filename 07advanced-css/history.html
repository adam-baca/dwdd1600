Birth to 8 weeks

Atlanta was born on February 14th, 2022 in Logan UT. Her mother is a sweet english cream and her father is a large and proud golden. She is 1 of 10 puppies. Her breeders took very good care of her and her siblings and are a loving family to the mother. Atlanta was the largest girl of the litter. We found her in march after our sweet puppy Gerogia passed away unexpectedly.
