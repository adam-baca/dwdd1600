Different Retriever types

There are a few different varieties of golden retrievers. All are loving and friendly!

VarietyPhysical FeaturesFur Color
AmericanSlim build, feathery fur.Gold
CanadianThe leanest build and tall, wiry fur.Red
BritishStocky and shorter, soft long coat.White
BrazilianNew and trending variety, large and stocky, wide boxy head with large paws, long fluffy golden coat.Gold
LabradorPeople often confuse labs with goldens, it is important to note that while they are retrievers, they are a slightly different breed with shorter hair and a stockier build. Still good boys.Yellow
